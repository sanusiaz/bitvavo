European

As a digital asset exchanges in the European Union, located in the The Netherlands, Bitvavo offers a highly connected platform with instant payment methods and SEPA support, making it the prime European gateway to digital assets.

Registered

Bitvavo is subject to AMLD5 legislation, GDPR and other regulations and operates in full compliance. Bitvavo is registered with the Dutch Central Bank (DNB) as a fiat to digital asset exchange and as a hosted wallet provider.

175+ Supported Assets

Exchange, transfer and store over 175 digital assets, directly with Euro. Bitvavo supports the wallet infrastructure for all listed digital assets.

Instant Funding Methods
